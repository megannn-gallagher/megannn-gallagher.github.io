Reliable Information Policy

This policy should be read alongside the Contributor Code of Conduct.

Any questions about this policy should be sent to reliableinfo-questions@example.com.

  1. Most importantly, this website is designed to share facts, not opinions. You must ensure your contributions do not, directly or indirectly, convey any opinions.
  2. You must not intentionally share any information which is false or misleading.
  3. You must take reasonable action to ensure that your contributions are true; you should fact check against multiple sources where possible.
  4. You must cite all sources you have used to obtain your information from, or check it against.
  5. Our volunteer auditors audit all contributions to this site. They reserve the right to reject any contribution which does not comply with the spirit or policies of this site.
